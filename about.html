About Us

 
 


Bradley Coleman has worked for more than two decades in the entertainment industry. His journey began in theatre, later expanding into the world of film and television. As a production designer, Bradley has art directed, styled, and built sets across the United States for a vast variety of clients including ABC, HGTV, DIY Network and many more.

Bradley founded Loop Scenic Studios Inc. in 2014 as a full-service scenic design studio. Loop Scenic Studios boasts a full-service art department offering an array of services including: prop building, prop procurement, demos, and small and large-scale sets.

We build and ship many of our projects across the United States, so being centrally located in Knoxville Tennessee is a huge plus. We have built and shipped over 100-morning segment sets to ABC’s Today Show in New York, and many of these projects have been staged in Times Square.

Loop Scenic Studios offers internal design services. We can also work with outside designers to help turn their vision into reality. Our aim is to support the overall artistic goals of the production.

We also work with contractors to create specialty projects in living spaces.  You dream it; we’ll build it.

Loop Scenic Studios looks forward to collaborating with you on your next project and making your dream a reality.
